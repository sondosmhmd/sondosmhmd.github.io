Chevrolet Blazer

Overview

The 2024 Chevrolet Blazer occupies an interesting middle ground
within the crowded mid-size SUV segment. It looks sporty and aggressive and has the responsive handling to back up
its styling, but it's also practical, featuring a spacious interior with
two rows of seats and a generously sized cargo area.
A turbocharged four-cylinder serves as the base engine,
and front-wheel drive is standard with all-wheel drive optional.
