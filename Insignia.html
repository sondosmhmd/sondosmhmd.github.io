Opel Insignia 2020 wagon


This generation of Opel Insignia is being produced from January, 2020. We can provide information about 8 modifications of this generation. 2020 Opel Insignia is available with four wheel (4x4) drive (2 versions) and front wheel drive (6 versions).


This Opel is available with three petrol engines with displacement 2.0 litres and power of from 170 to 230 horsepower un two diesel engines with displacement from 1.5 to 2.0 litres and power of from 122 to 174 horsepower.


Opel Insignia 2020 wagon average fuel consumption in combined cycle is from 4.2 to 5.1 litres per 100km. The most fuel efficient Opel Insignia 2020 wagon version is Opel Insignia 1.5d, consuming 4.2 litres of diesel per 100km.


The best acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h - 7.6 seconds has Opel Insignia GSi 2.0 version, conversely the tardiest one is Opel Insignia 1.5d Automatic reaching this speed only in 12.4 seconds. The maximum top speed is 235 km/h for the 2.0 petrol 4-cylinder 230 hp version (Opel Insignia GSi 2.0).


Lenght of Opel Insignia is 500 cm, it is large car. For comparison - Opel Insignia is about 4 cm longer than BMW 5 series estate car / wagon. Opel Insignia gross weight is from 2200 to 2330 kg (depending on modification), which is about 20 percent lighter than the average for this age and class. Opel Insignia trunk space is 560 litres, by folding down all rear seats boot capacity can be increased to 1665 litres.
