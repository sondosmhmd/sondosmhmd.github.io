ECO MODE

Eco Mode helps improve fuel economy. Automatically engaged when you start the vehicle, it activates the Quadra-Lift® air suspension system and lowers it when the vehicle speed exceeds 55 mph. It also alters the shift schedule of the 8-speed transmission and front-to-rear torque split to increase efficiency even further.

QUADRA-LIFT AIR SUSPENSION SYSTEM

This revolutionary system gives you ultimate control of your suspension by raising your vehicle to a maximum ground clearance of 280 mm, which is 60 mm higher than normal ride height. It also alters the shift schedule of the transmission and front-to-rear torque split to increase efficiency even further..
